How it works

1

Search

Type a word or phrase in PeekDrive. It finds every file that contains your search and shows you the matching passages.

2

Preview

See the matching passage right in the results, with context around your search. For spreadsheets, a mini-table shows the matching row and column.

3

Open

Click "Open" and land directly on the right paragraph in a Doc, the right cell in a Sheet, or the right slide in a presentation.

Why PeekDrive

Passage preview

See the matching text in context without opening the file. You get the result and its surroundings instantly.

Direct access

One click takes you to the exact paragraph, cell, or slide. Your search term is automatically copied to your clipboard.

Filters and sorting

Narrow results by file type (Docs, Sheets, Slides, PDF) and sort by date or relevance.

Browse all matches

Click "See other results" to navigate through every matching passage within a single file.

100% private

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data sent to external servers. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies.

Free

PeekDrive is free. Install the extension and start searching.
